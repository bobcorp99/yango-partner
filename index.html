What the income can be?
The final salary of the driver depends on the region and on the work schedule. With Yango.Taxi you can both moonlight for just a few hours a week and carry out orders every day. And for drivers in the service has its own bonuses. With income, everything is transparent. In the "Earnings" section in Yango.Pro you can check the list of all trips and detailed information on each: route, cost, bonuses. Passengers will pay you both in cash and by card. And sometimes leave a tip :)
How do I receive orders with Yango Pro?
To start working as a driver with the Yango.Taxi service is very simple. You need a smartphone with Android 5.0 operating system, camera, GPS-module and 2 GB of RAM. Download and install the Yango.Pro app. It will offer you orders, lay the route, calculate the price of the trip and keep you informed about all the news of the service. You won't have to search for passengers or think about the best way to get around - you can concentrate on the orders.
Can I choose my working conditions?
Yes, you can work part-time, fulfilling orders only during peak hours, or spend the whole working day on the line - it's up to you. And Yandex.Pro will also highlight for you purple areas where the demand for rides is higher, and will offer orders on the way home at the end of your shift, so you don't waste gasoline idly.
What kind of car is suitable for service work?
It all depends on the rate at which you want to fulfill orders. We take into account the model, year of manufacture (from 2000) of the car and its average market value. Only cars with at least 4 doors are suitable for work.
